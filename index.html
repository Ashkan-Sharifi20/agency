( 04 ) — FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Contact us
What makes Shxrifi Studio different from other web agencies?
We hand-build every site from scratch — no templates, no page builders. You get a website that's genuinely yours, designed around your brand and built with clean custom code that loads fast and lasts.
How much does a website cost?
It depends on scope. A clean multi-page site typically starts around $700, with e-commerce and custom features priced higher. We'll give you a clear quote upfront — no surprises. Optional monthly maintenance is available to keep everything running.
How long does it take to build?
Most projects take one to three weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on the number of pages and features. We keep you updated throughout and never disappear mid-project.
Do you handle hosting and the domain?
Yes. We can set up your domain and hosting and manage everything for you, so you never have to touch the technical side. It's all included in our optional care plan.
What happens after my site launches?
We stick around. With a care plan we handle updates, fixes, and changes whenever you need them — new menu items, photos, hours, anything. Your site stays fresh without you lifting a finger.
